Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know which course is right for me?

We offer a free course assessment to help determine your skill level and learning goals. Alternatively, you can browse our course categories (Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced) or contact our team for personalized recommendations.

Do I need to own a piano to take your courses?

While having access to a piano or keyboard is necessary for practice, you don't need to own one immediately. Many beginners start with a digital keyboard, and we provide recommendations for various budgets in our resources section.

How long will it take to learn piano?

Learning piano is a journey that varies based on your goals, practice time, and prior musical experience. Many students begin playing simple songs within a few weeks, while mastery takes years of dedicated practice. Our courses are designed to provide clear milestones and steady progress.

Do you offer refunds if I'm not satisfied?

Yes, we offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied with your course purchase, you can request a full refund within the first 30 days of enrollment.
